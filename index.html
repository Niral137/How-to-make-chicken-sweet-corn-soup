How to make chicken sweet corn soup?

There are many ways to make chicken sweet corn soup, but out of all the recipes I think this is the easiest recipe.


Ingredients for Sweet Corn Chicken Soup

•350 g Chicken cooked, shredded
•500 g Sweet Corn Kernels cooked
•900 ml Chicken Stock
•800 ml Water
•2 tblsp Red Capsicum sliced
•as per taste White Pepper Powder
•as per taste Salt
•3 to 4 Eggs lightly beaten
•handful Coriander Leaves chopped
•1/4 cup Corn Flour
•2 tblsp Cream
•handful Spring Onions chopped


How to make Sweet Corn Chicken Soup

1•Mix the cream and sweet corn in a bowl. Keep aside until required.
2•Mix the corn flour with 1/3 cup of water and stir well.
3•Heat a large pan over medium flame.
4•Pour the water and chicken stock.
5•Bring to a boil and reduce flame to low.
6•Add the shredded chicken and simmer for a few minutes.
7•Remove any foam that rises to the surface.
8•Add the sweet corn, pepper powder, salt and the corn flour mixture.
9•Stir well and add the beaten eggs.
10•Stir gently and simmer for a minute.
11•Switch off the flame.
12•Pour into individual soup bowls.
13•Garnish with spring onions and coriander leaves.
14•Serve!
